A portal for Orphanage Donation Managements

Bella-Casa"- a home to those who don't have one,a faith for those in search of a hope and a family to those who are devoid of one. Bella-Casa (derived from Italian words: bella meaning beautiful & casa meaning home) is a beautiful home for orphans. We provide a friendly environment with members being treated as a family, to nurture an orphan and help him create a family in the world of complete strange faces. Education, moral values, sports, helping others- every quality is further added to the little hearts of these childre, so that they can cope up with this world beautifully and with an essence that prevails.
